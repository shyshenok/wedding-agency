Wedding cake

We will help you choose and arrange a wedding cake in accordance with your ideas and to complement the style and theme of the wedding.

  • We will arrange a consultation and tasting with a professional pastry chef
  • Options of fondant or marzipan cakes with different choices of fillings
  • Possibilities of adding fresh fruits such as raspberries, strawberries, bananas, forest fruit or sour cherries in alcohol
  • Satin or marzipan ribbons around the cake
  • Your wedding cake can be decorated with fresh or marzipan flowers, dots, fruits or personalized figures

Your wedding cake will not only look great, but taste great!

DECORATION

We can talk for hours about the wedding decorations! We love this part of wedding planning and we constantly watch for new trends. We will advise you with decorative ideas for your wedding ceremony and reception including colours, creative touches, lighting and flowers/centerpieces.

The service includes:

  • Meeting the couple, filling in the questionnaire, identifying wishes and taste preferences in design.
  • Creating the style of the wedding
  • Managing and controlling of the project
  • Planning the budget, providing the transparent reporting on the decoration and floristics
  • Control and implementation of the design project on the wedding day
